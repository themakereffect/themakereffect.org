Donate to Support The Maker Effect Foundation

The Maker Effect Foundation programs such as Maker Faire Orlando and MakerFX Makerspace are made possible through generous donations from our local community, including individuals, companies, and organizations.

Donate using PayPal

Use the button below to be redirected to PayPal to make a donation with a credit card or other PayPal payment method.





Donate via Check or Other Form of Payment

To donate via check or other form of payment, please email treasurer@themakereffect.org.

In-kind Donations

In-kind donations of products or services are welcomed. please email community@themakereffect.org to discuss.

Volunteer

To support TMEF programs by volunteering, email us at volunteer@themakereffect.org to discuss volunteer opportunties.

Sponsor

If your organization supports STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts & Math) education, community arts, or other community impact objectives, you can make a huge impact by sponsoring the programs of The Maker Effect Foundation. We work with our sponsors to match TMEF programs to your community objectives, and to create tailored activations that engage the local community with sponsor brands and products.

For more information on sponsoring TMEF programs, use the contact form below, or email us at community@themakereffect.org
